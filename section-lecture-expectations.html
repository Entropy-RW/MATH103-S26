    Note 5.1. Taking Notes.

    I may not provide any notes, slides, or additional materials. It is your responsibility to take notes during lectures. Developing strong note-taking skills is an important and useful academic ability that will benefit you throughout your studies and professional career. Taking effective notes helps you actively engage with the material, improve comprehension, and retain information more efficiently. This course is an opportunity for you to practice and strengthen these skills, which are essential for independent learning.
